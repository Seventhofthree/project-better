# Information Architecture

## Golden Rule

Every piece of information has one home. Other screens may display it, but data should not be duplicated.

## Core Modules

### Home

Purpose: Tell Joshua what matters today.

Contains:
- Today's Big Three
- Morning Routine
- Night Routine
- Today's Workout
- Today's Meals
- Current Weight
- Next Milestone
- Upcoming reminders

### Health

Purpose: Manage body care and health maintenance.

Contains:
- Weight
- Grooming
- Sleep
- Medical
- Dental
- Vision
- Measurements
- Medications / supplements

### Nutrition

Purpose: Remove food decisions.

Contains:
- Meal plans
- Recipes
- Shopping list
- Pantry
- Meal prep
- Nutrition goals

### Fitness

Purpose: Guide today's workout and track progression.

Contains:
- Workout library
- Today's workout
- Progression
- Equipment
- Mobility
- Personal records

### Progress

Purpose: Show whether the system is working.

Contains:
- Weight graph
- Habit streaks
- Monthly reviews
- Milestones
- Photos
- Achievements

### Settings

Purpose: Configure Pathfinder.

Contains:
- Theme
- Notifications
- Backup
- Account / local profile
- About

## Page Types

### Action Pages

Used to complete something:
- Morning Routine
- Night Routine
- Today's Workout
- Log Weight

### Reference Pages

Used to read instructions:
- Grooming routine
- Meal plan
- Workout instructions

### History Pages

Used to review progress:
- Weight history
- Streaks
- Monthly reviews
